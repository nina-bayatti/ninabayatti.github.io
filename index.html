To the Trainers Who Inspired Me . . .

Six months ago, I began my fitness journey thanks to an unlimited ClassPass membership and a coworker-turned-fitness-buddy. To get the most out of my expensive membership, I began a rigorous two-a-days lifestyle, taking fitness classes before and after my 9-to-5.

What I've learned during my fitness journey is the impact of a great fitness trainer. In an effort to help other gym class addicts, I've created a platform to share stories of the trainers who take the time to inspire a healthy change in their students.
