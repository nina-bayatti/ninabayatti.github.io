About Me

The Evolution of a Gym Class Addict

In April of 2016, I signed up for an unlimited ClassPass membership. ClassPass offers members access to an assortment of large-chain and boutique gym fitness classes for a flat membership fee.

To ensure I was getting the most out of my membership, I began a rigorous two-a-days fitness regimine.

And what I've learned during my fitness journey is the impact of a great fitness trainer. While spa amenities and new gym equipment are enjoyable, the trainer has the biggest impact on your class experience.

Why I Created "Find a Trainer"

I've taken over 250 fitness classes in NYC. Some of these trainers have become my friends, role models, and motivators.

This website is to help fellow gym addicts alike discover new classes and trainers in the NYC area.

This website also serves as a THANK YOU to all of the trainers who have been a part of my fitness journey. I appreciate your energy, positivity, and support. You've made me stronger, faster, and better.
